DocFerry 0.0.15

This release supersedes `0.0.14` and publishes the June 30 public free launch plugin.

**Included:**

- Current DocFerry dashboard/settings UI.
- Fuyonder account connection.
- Upload disclosure before publishing vault content.
- Request-access flow for reviewed Plus access while public billing remains disabled.
- Share, import, stop-sharing, and linked-status workflows.
- Free-launch access policy integration with the hosted DocFerry service.

**Public billing:**

- Checkout and public paid plans remain disabled in this release.
- Approved access upgrades are handled through request review.

**Release assets:**

- `main.js`
- `manifest.json`
- `styles.css`
- `docferry-obsidian-plugin-0.0.15.zip`
